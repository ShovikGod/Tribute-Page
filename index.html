Timeline

  • 1960 - Born in Lanús, Buenos Aires Province
  • 1976 - Debut for Argentinos Juniors at the age of 15 with number 16 jersey.He score his first goal in the Primera División against Marplatense team San Lorenzo on 14 November
  • 1976 to 1981 - Spents five years at Argentinos Juniors scoring 115 goals in 167 appearances
  • 1981 - Signs a contract with Boca Juniors. Plays his first "Superclásico" against River Plate at La Bombonera and scores a goal
  • 1982 - Signs a contract with Barcelona. He plays his first World Cup Tournament in Spain
  • 1983 - Wins Copa del Rey (Spain's annual national cup competition). Defeats Real Madrid in "El Clasico" scoring and become the firs Barcelona player to be applauded by arch rival Real Madrid fans. He had broken ankle caused by ill-timed tackle by by Athletic Bilbao's Andoni Goikoetxea
  • 1984 - During Copa del Rey final he gets in a fight with Goikoetxea after receiving another rough tackle, causing a mass brawl eruption.
    He demands to be trasferred out of Camp Nou.
    He signs with Napoli, where he is welcomed from Naples supporters as their saviour
  • 1986 - Lead Argentina to win The World Cup Tournament scoring the greatest goal in history of the World Cup, according to FIFA. He wins the Golden Ball
  • 1987 - He leads Napoli to win their first Serie A Italian Championship. He wins "Coppa Italia". He is the top scorer in Serie A
  • 1989 - Wins UEFA Cup
  • 1990 - He leads Napoli to win their first Serie A Italian Championship. He wins Italian Supercup
  • 1992 - Left Napoli after being banned for failing a drug test for cocaine and signs with Sevilla
  • 1993 - Plays for Newell's Old Boys
  • 1994 - Plays in only 2 games, scoring one goal against Greece, before being sent home after failing a drug test for ephedrine doping. He will argue that the test result was due to his personal trainer giving him the power drink Rip Fuel.
  • 1995 - Returns to Boca Juniors for 2 years
  • 2000 - Publishes his autobiography "Yo Soy El Diego". He wins FIFA Player of the Century award
  • 2005 - Return to Boca Juniors as a sports vice president and wins Apertura, Copa Sudamericana, Recopa Sudamericana
  • 2006 - Wins Clausura
  • 2008 - Becomes coach of Argentina for the first time
  • 2011 - Becomes manager of Dubai club AI WASL FC
  • 2014 - Takes part in the "Match for Peace" in Rome
  • 2015 - Maradona's father, "Don" Diego, died on 25 June, 2015 at age 87.
  • 2017 - Head coach of Fujairah
  • 2018 - In September 2018 Maradona was appointed manager of Mexican second division side Dorados.
  • 2019 - On 5 September 2019, Maradona was unveiled as the new head coach of Gimnasia de La Plata
    On 5 September, Maradona became the President Gimnisia de La Plata
  • 2020 - On November 2, Maradona was admitted to a hospital in La Plata
    On 25 November 2020, at the age of 60, Maradona died of a heart attack at his home in Tigre, Buenos Aires Province, Argentina.
